# Grade Tracker  
## Smart tool for managing your academic performance

Tired of asking teachers about your grades?  
Want to see your current performance anytime?  
Want to understand where you need to improve and where you’re doing well?  
Want to know in advance the minimum scores you need to achieve your goal?  
And keep your data fully private?  
Confused by different grading formulas for each subject?  

**Grade Tracker solves all of these problems.**

This is not just a grade storage app —  
it is a system that helps students **understand, control, and plan their academic results**.

---

## 💡 Concept

The application acts as a bridge (buffer) between teachers and official academic systems.

It simplifies the workflow for teachers and at the same time gives students a powerful tool to track and analyze their performance.

---

## 👨‍🏫 Teacher Features

- Manage grades in a simple electronic journal  
- Edit grades freely without bureaucracy  
- Import data from Excel  
- Work in “draft mode” before publishing  
- Publish grades only after verification  

---

## 👨‍🎓 Student Features

- Access only personal grades  
- Track progress for each subject  
- Get predicted final grades  
- Understand what scores are needed to reach a goal  

---

## 🔐 Privacy — Key Advantage

In traditional systems, grades are often shared as full lists, which can affect student privacy.

In Grade Tracker:

- Each student has individual access (via email)  
- Students can see only their own grades  
- No comparison with others  

This:

- Reduces social pressure  
- Makes studying more comfortable  
- Helps students focus on their own progress
